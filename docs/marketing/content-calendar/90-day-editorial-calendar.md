## Pillar weighting

Distribution across the 24 evergreen posts:

| Pillar | Posts | % | Why this weight |
|---|---|---|---|
| Red Flags & Scams | 6 | 25% | Highest commercial intent. People searching "tinder red flags" or "romance scam signs" are warm — they've already had a bad experience or are about to. These convert. |
| Tools & How-To | 5 | 21% | Drives signups directly because the post itself demos the product. Also captures branded ("how to install SafeTea") and feature-led ("reverse image search dating profile") queries that competitors can't compete on. |
| AWDTSG Successor | 4 | 17% | The warmest cohort we have. Anyone searching AWDTSG terms knows the movement and is bought into the premise. Lower volume on some queries, but conversion rate is exceptional. |
| Safety Education | 4 | 17% | Top-of-funnel. "First date safety tips" pulls broad traffic and we earn brand authority by having the most useful answer. Internal links push readers down to product pages. |
| Privacy Deep Dives | 3 | 13% | Differentiator content. Most safety apps can't credibly write these — we can. Lower direct conversion, but they earn backlinks from privacy and tech press, which lifts every other post. |
| Founder/Brand | 2 | 8% | Trust-building. Two posts in 90 days is enough — more dilutes signal. These also get repurposed into TikTok / Reels / press. |

**Why no SEO-only "best dating apps 2026" cash-grab content:** that traffic doesn't convert for a safety platform. We are not a dating-app-comparison site, and ranking for those queries trains Google to send us the wrong intent.

## Calendar maintenance rules

1. **Last-updated stamps:** every post gets a `last_updated` field in front matter and is reviewed quarterly. Outdated red-flag posts are worse than no post.
2. **Internal-link audit:** before publishing post N, add at least one inbound link from posts N-3, N-6, and N-9 if topically relevant. The cluster compounds.
3. **One feature drop per pillar per quarter:** when SafeWalk, AI Screening, or another feature ships an upgrade, the next post in the matching pillar references it explicitly.
4. **Kill criteria:** any post under 200 organic visits/month at the 90-day mark gets a rewrite, not a delete. Rewrites preserve the URL and lift the cluster.
5. **No iOS download CTAs.** Period. Per `ALTERNATIVE_MARKETING_PLAN.md`, every CTA points to a web destination (`/signup`, `/dashboard`, `/install`, `/city/<slug>`, etc.).
