How It Works

  1. Check Out — Upload a photo, enter the location, how you're getting there, and add trusted contacts.
  2. SafeTea Report — A shareable safety report is generated with all your trip details.
  3. Share It — Send the report via SMS or through the SafeTea inbox to your trusted contacts.
  4. Live Tracking — Your contacts can see your status in real time. If you're overdue, they'll know.
  5. Check In — When you're done, tap "Check In — I'm Safe" and everyone gets a confirmation.
