What SafeTea does

SafeTea is a privacy-first personal safety platform for women. Every account is tied to a verified human — no AI-generated photos, no stock images, no bots. Our core tools:

  • SafeTea Check-In — share your plans with trusted contacts via SMS, with live tracking during a date or meetup.
  • SafeTea Pulse — real-time session monitoring that detects inactivity, missed check-ins, and unexpected route changes, then escalates to your emergency contacts.
  • SOS Record & Protect — discreet audio recording and location alerts, triggered from the Tools tab.
  • SafeLink — temporary live-location sharing with anyone, for rides or walks.
  • Safety Vault — end-to-end encrypted journaling, photo/audio/video documentation, and trusted-contact release. A SafeTea+ feature.
  • Name Watch — monitor names across SafeTea community posts and get alerts when a name is mentioned.
  • Tea Talk — Safety Support Chat — safety-only community feed moderated for accuracy.
  • AI-powered Conversation Scanner — paste a profile or chat and our AI analyzes it for manipulation tactics and red flags.

Built on verified identity

Every SafeTea account passes a selfie + liveness challenge before accessing community features. We do not store biometric data or ID images — verification data is processed and permanently deleted immediately after review. We retain only the pass/fail result and a timestamp.

This is what makes SafeTea different: you are always talking to a real, verified human.

Founders & team
