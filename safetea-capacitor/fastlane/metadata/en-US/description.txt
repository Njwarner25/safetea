SafeTea is a personal safety platform that helps you feel safer when meeting someone new. Verify identities, check names against community reports, and send emergency alerts to trusted contacts.

KEY FEATURES:

Community Safety Feed
Join city-based safety communities where members share moderated safety experiences and recommendations. All content is reviewed to ensure it meets community guidelines.

Identity Verification
Verify your identity with AI-powered selfie + liveness checks. See trust scores for other community members based on their verification status.

SafeWalk Check-In
Share your plans and location with trusted contacts via SMS. Real-time location tracking lets your contacts know where you are until you mark yourself safe.

Emergency SOS
One-tap emergency features including fake incoming call, discreet audio recording with location sharing, and direct 911 access.

Photo Verification
Upload screenshots and our AI analyzes them for signs of deception — metadata checks, editing detection, and reverse image search.

Conversation Scanner
Paste a conversation and our AI identifies manipulation tactics, love bombing, gaslighting, and other warning signs.

Private Sorority Rooms
Create invite-only spaces for your chapter sisters to share safety insights and recommendations in a trusted, private environment.

Area Safety Alerts
Real-time crime data from public safety sources for your city, filtered to show incidents most relevant to personal safety.

SafeTea+ Subscription ($7.99/month or $66.99/year)
Unlock premium features including Name Watch alerts, unlimited photo verifications, advanced SOS tools, and priority community features.

All user-submitted content is moderated and should not be considered factual claims about any individual. SafeTea is a safety awareness tool — not a background check service or law enforcement database.
