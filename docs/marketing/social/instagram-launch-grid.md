# Instagram Launch Grid — @safe_teaapp

**Format:** First 12 feed posts arranged as a 3×4 grid, designed to be read top-to-bottom-left-to-right by a new visitor scrolling the profile. Each post stands alone but the grid as a whole tells the brand story: *we exist, here's why, here's what we do, here's how to start*.

**Visual system:**
- Palette: warm off-white background (#F6F1EA), deep teal accent (#1F4E4A), soft coral highlight (#E89A7B). No stock photos of women looking afraid. No padlock emojis. No app-store badges (iOS approval pending).
- Typography: one serif headline face + one clean sans for body. Consistent across all 12 posts.
- All faces are illustrated, never real photos. This is a brand promise, enforced visually from post one.

**CTA convention:** Caption ends with "**safetea.app — open in any browser**" (no iOS download CTAs, anywhere). Profile bio handles the link.

**Hashtag strategy:** mix of high-volume discovery tags + mid-volume niche tags + 1 brand tag (`#SafeTea`). Cap at 8 per post; 5 is fine.

## Ops notes

- **Cadence:** post 3 per week for the first four weeks (Mon / Wed / Fri at 11am ET) to seat the full grid before any paid amplification.
- **Reels priority:** posts 3, 6, 9 are Reels and should be cross-promoted as Stories with stickers ("new on the grid").
- **Story complement:** none of the grid posts depend on Stories, but a daily question-sticker Story ("what's your dating-safety green flag?") feeds engagement without cluttering the feed.
- **Comments policy:** respond to first-week comments within 4 hours. After week one, daily check-ins are fine. Never engage with bad-faith comments publicly — hide, don't argue.
- **Saved-posts target:** posts 5, 6, 8, 11 are designed to be *saved*. Save rate is the leading indicator we'll track for grid effectiveness, not likes.
