## Journalist FAQ

**1. Isn't this just a defamation lawsuit waiting to happen?**
SafeTea was built with legal counsel from day one. Reports are structured around personal experience, not accusations of crimes, and our terms of service draw clear lines. We have a takedown and dispute process, professional moderation, and we cooperate with valid legal process. The risk profile is meaningfully different from an unmoderated Facebook group.

**2. How is this different from the Tea app?**
Tea verifies real identities and surfaces real names. SafeTea is pseudonyms-only, with illustrated avatars instead of photos, on both sides of every report. The categorical difference is privacy by design: we don't collect what could be weaponized.

**3. How do you prevent abuse — fake reports, vendettas, harassment campaigns?**
Three layers: identity verification at signup (verified that you're real, not who you are), professional human moderation augmented by AI classification, and a community trust system that weights reports by reporter history. We also actively monitor for coordinated inauthentic behavior.

**4. What stops men from joining and screenshotting?**
We require gender verification at signup, run continuous abuse-detection on accounts, and the platform has technical controls to deter screenshotting. We are honest that no system is perfect — we tell users to assume any platform can leak and to share accordingly.

**5. Is this only for women?**
SafeTea was built for women dating men, which is the cohort with the documented safety gap. We're studying how to extend safely to other communities (queer dating, trans-inclusive safety) and will only ship those when we can do it without compromising the core community.

**6. What happens if someone reports a person who's done nothing wrong?**
We have a dispute process. The reported person can submit a challenge through a verified channel; reports that fail moderation review or violate our policies are removed. We do not take a "always believe the post" position — we take a "structured, evidenced, moderated" position.

**7. Why web-first instead of an app?**
The web app works for everyone today, on any device, with no app store gatekeeping. It's also more privacy-respecting — fewer device permissions to request, less data to collect. We have native apps in the pipeline, but the web is the product.

**8. How big are you?**
We're live across <<VERIFY-CITY-COUNT>> cities with <<VERIFY-USER-COUNT>> users. We're growing primarily through word of mouth and the AWDTSG community. We don't share precise growth figures publicly because we don't think they're the most important measure of impact.

**9. How do you make money?**
SafeTea+ is an optional paid subscription with extended features. The free tier is fully functional for safety use cases — we will not paywall safety. We do not sell user data, will not run targeted ads against safety content, and don't intend to.
