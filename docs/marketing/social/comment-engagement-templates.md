# SafeTea — 20 Comment Templates for Daily Engagement

**Purpose:** Time-saver for the operator. When you scroll target creators' posts (dating coaches, women's safety advocates, AWDTSG-aware accounts), drop one of these in 30 seconds instead of writing from scratch each time.

**The single most important rule:** **Add value first.** A comment that contributes to the conversation gets surfaced by the algorithm and read by the creator's audience. A comment that pitches SafeTea gets buried, reported as spam, or gets your account flagged. Most of the templates below are pure value-add with **zero brand mention**. The brand-mention ones are clearly labeled — use them sparingly (max 2/week) and only after you have karma in the community.

**Where to use:** TikTok comments, Instagram comments, IG Reels comments. Not Twitter/X (different game). Not Reddit (use the Reddit playbook instead).

**Posting cadence:** 10 comments/day, 5 days/week = 50/week. Don't comment-spam in a single sitting; spread across the day so the algorithm doesn't flag bot-like behavior.

## DO / DON'T

**DO:**
- Read the post before commenting. Generic "🔥🔥🔥" gets nothing; specific reactions get pinned by creators.
- Vary your phrasing. Don't use the same template twice on the same creator's account.
- Reply to other commenters, not just the creator. Threaded replies = compound visibility.
- When you mention SafeTea, always disclose ("disclosure: founder," "I work on SafeTea," etc.).

**DON'T:**
- Don't drop links in comments. TikTok and IG suppress link-bearing comments. The bio link is the funnel.
- Don't comment in pure marketing voice. If a real person wouldn't write it, don't post it.
- Don't pile 5 comments on the same creator's post. One thoughtful one per post, max.
- Don't argue with hostile commenters. Disengage. Your goal is visibility, not victory.
- Don't comment on creator posts that are about something traumatic (assault stories, etc.) with anything brand-adjacent. Just don't be there.

## Daily quota recommendation

- **5 minutes, morning:** scroll your TikTok For You / IG Reels / IG explore → leave 5 Group A or B comments on red-flag / safety posts
- **5 minutes, evening:** repeat with 5 more on different creators
- **Once per week, Friday:** 1–2 Group D brand-mention comments on the highest-fit posts you saved during the week

That's it. 20-line job per day; visibility builds in 2–3 weeks if you're consistent.
