What is an Outcry Witness?

You may be what's legally known as an "outcry witness" — the first person a victim discloses abuse to. In many states, your testimony as an outcry witness carries special evidentiary weight in court. This recording may serve as critical evidence.

What to do: Save this page. Try to contact them. If no response, call 911.
