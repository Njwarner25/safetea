SafeTea Pulse

Real-time safety awareness during active sessions. Pulse watches for anomalies while you're walking, on a date, or headed somewhere — and alerts your trusted contact if something seems off.

What Pulse watches for

Inactivity

No movement for longer than expected — Pulse checks in.

Route changes

Unexpected detour away from your destination.

Missed check-ins

Your scheduled check-in window passed without a response.

Session overruns

Your trip is running much longer than planned.

How it works

"Are you okay?"

If something seems off, Pulse prompts you with vibration and a countdown.

No response → alert sent

Your trusted contact gets your name, location, and anomaly type automatically.

Safe Zones

Inside trusted locations like home or work, Pulse pauses on its own.

Get Pulse on your phone

Download for Android

Install the latest build directly. Pulse runs in the Tools tab once the app is open.

Download APK
Install steps
  1. Tap the button above on your Android phone.
  2. Open the downloaded .apk file.
  3. If prompted, allow "Install from unknown sources" for your browser.
  4. Open SafeTea → Tools tab → tap SafeTea Pulse.
