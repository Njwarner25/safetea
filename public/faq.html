What does SafeTea do?

SafeTea is a personal safety platform for women, built around these tools:

  • SafeTea Check-In — share your plans with trusted contacts via SMS, with live tracking.
  • SafeTea Pulse — real-time session monitoring that detects inactivity, missed check-ins, and route changes.
  • SOS Record & Protect — discreet audio recording + location alerts on emergency trigger.
  • SafeLink — temporary live-location sharing for rides or walks.
  • Safety Vault — encrypted journaling + trusted-contact release for documenting abuse.
  • Name Watch — alerts when a name you're watching is mentioned in the community.
  • Conversation Scanner — AI red-flag analysis of chats and profiles.
  • Tea Talk — Safety Support Chat — safety-only community feed.
How does identity verification work?

Every SafeTea account completes a selfie and liveness challenge before accessing community features. Verification data is processed and permanently deleted immediately after review — we store only the pass/fail result and a timestamp. No biometrics, no ID images, no social credentials.

Is SafeTea free?

Core safety tools — Check-In, SafeLink, Pulse, SOS, community feed — are free. SafeTea+ is an optional subscription ($7.99/month or $66.99/year, 30% savings annually) that unlocks the Safety Vault, Name Watch, extra photo scans, priority support, and premium screening tools.

Is SafeTea a dating app?

No. SafeTea is not a dating app. It's a safety companion that works alongside your existing dating, ride-hailing, and social apps — giving you live tracking, check-ins, and verified-community insight so you can feel safer on any plan.

Who founded SafeTea?

SafeTea is veteran-founded and built in consultation with survivors, trauma-informed-care practitioners, and gender-based-violence response experts. The team combines backgrounds in military service, trust & safety engineering, and survivor advocacy.

How does SafeTea protect my data?

Safety Vault entries and files are end-to-end encrypted with a per-folder key wrapped by a server master key. SafeTea staff cannot read your vault contents. Community posts are AI-moderated for names, accusations, and doxxing. SafeTea does not sell user data.
