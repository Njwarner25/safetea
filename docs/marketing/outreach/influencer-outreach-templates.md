# SafeTea Influencer & Creator Outreach Templates

**Owner:** Partnerships Lead
**Last updated:** 2026-04-27
**Use:** Send-ready templates for outreach to dating coaches, women's safety creators, and sex/relationship educators in the 50k–500k follower range.

**Operating principles:**

1. **Gift first, ask never on the cold.** First contact offers value, requests nothing.
2. **Pseudonyms-only on all creative.** Never reference any real person's dating history, name, or photo, even in a script.
3. **FTC compliance is non-negotiable.** Every paid post requires #ad and a clear material-connection disclosure. No "thanks to the brand"; a clear "paid partnership with SafeTea."
4. **No iOS download CTAs while approval is pending.** Always direct creators to safetea.app (web) or PWA install instructions.
5. **Brand voice:** smart, protective, calm. Never fearmongering. Treat creators' audiences as adults.

## Partnership Terms — One-Pager

### Compensation tiers

Flat fee scales by follower size on the creator's primary platform. Per-signup payout is constant across tiers.

| Tier | Follower range (primary platform) | Flat fee per deliverable bundle | Per-signup payout |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tier A | 50,000 – 100,000 | $750 | $3 per verified signup |
| Tier B | 100,001 – 250,000 | $1,750 | $3 per verified signup |
| Tier C | 250,001 – 500,000 | $3,500 | $3 per verified signup |
| Tier D (case-by-case) | 500,000+ | Quoted per engagement | $3 per verified signup |

**Deliverable bundle (default):**

- 1× short-form video (TikTok or Reel, 30–90 seconds)
- 1× Story sequence (3–5 frames) on the same platform within 7 days
- 1× pinned comment with the referral link for 30 days
- License for SafeTea to repost the video on owned channels (paid social and organic) for 90 days, with creator credit

**Bonus structures:**

- $500 bonus if the primary video crosses 250,000 organic views in 30 days
- $1,500 bonus if it crosses 1,000,000 organic views in 30 days
- Quarterly "top creator" bonus of $2,000 to the highest per-signup performer in each tier

**Payment:** flat fee paid 50% on script approval, 50% on publish. Per-signup payouts paid monthly, net 30, against a verified signup report from the SafeTea attribution dashboard.

**Term:** 90-day initial engagement; renewable by mutual agreement.

### FTC compliance — non-negotiable

Every sponsored deliverable must include:

1. **In-caption disclosure:** "#ad" or "Paid partnership with SafeTea" at the top of the caption, not buried.
2. **In-video disclosure:** the words "paid partnership" or "ad" visible on screen within the first 3 seconds, AND said out loud at least once.
3. **Platform's native paid-partnership tag** enabled where available (Instagram Branded Content tools, TikTok Branded Content toggle).
4. **Material-connection language** in the script (a clear "SafeTea is paying me to talk about this" beat — not buried).

Posts that fail these requirements will be considered out of contract; SafeTea reserves the right to withhold the flat fee until they are corrected.

### Brand safety — what we will not approve

- Any reference to a real person's name, photo, or dating profile (even hypothetically).
- Fearmongering framing ("most men are dangerous," "you'll be assaulted if you don't").
- Outcome guarantees ("SafeTea will catch the catfish," "you're safe with SafeTea").
- iOS App Store download CTAs (web URL only: safetea.app, or PWA install instructions).
- Content that targets minors or features anyone under 18.

## Talking Points — In Creator Voice

**Hand this to creators after the gift period. Five SafeTea differentiators they can ad-lib in their own voice. Not a script — a list of true things.**

1. **No real names. No real photos. Ever.** SafeTea uses pseudonyms and illustrated avatars on every report. The platform is structurally incapable of doxxing because it doesn't collect what doxxing requires.

2. **Built as the AWDTSG successor — without the Facebook problems.** No groups disappearing overnight. No screenshots leaking to the person being discussed. No volunteer moderators getting sued. Professional moderation, structured posts, legal framework, all of it built in from day one.

3. **Web-first, no app store needed.** Open safetea.app in any browser on any phone. iOS users can add it to their home screen and it works like an app. No download, no permissions, no friction.

4. **Women-led, women-built.** The leadership team and engineering team are women. The product was designed by the people who need it, not by people guessing at what they need.

5. **Privacy is the architecture, not a setting.** SafeTea doesn't sell data, doesn't run targeted ads against safety content, and limits collection to what's required to operate. The free tier is fully functional — safety is not paywalled.

**Useful one-liners creators can borrow:**

- "AWDTSG proved we needed this. SafeTea is what it should have been from the start."
- "Date smarter, stay safer."
- "The platform that can't dox you because it never had your name."
- "Built by women who got tired of waiting for the dating apps to fix this."

**Things creators should NOT say (we will reject the post if they do):**

- "SafeTea will keep you safe." (No outcome guarantees. Say "SafeTea is a tool that helps you make informed decisions.")
- "Most men are dangerous." (Not our voice. Not true. Don't go there.)
- "Download the app." (We are web-first; the iOS app is not currently available. Say "open safetea.app in your browser.")
- "I bet [specific celebrity / public figure] is on there." (No real names. Ever.)

## DO / DON'T List

### DO

- **DO disclose paid partnership clearly** — #ad or "Paid partnership with SafeTea" at the top of the caption, on-screen within the first 3 seconds, said out loud once.
- **DO use your custom referral link** — it's how we attribute signups and pay you per-signup bonuses.
- **DO direct viewers to safetea.app** — the web URL works on every device.
- **DO use your own voice** — your audience came for you, not for our brand voice.
- **DO redact any real-world examples** — names, photos, screenshots with identifying info, all blurred or replaced.
- **DO send us the script for review** before publishing — 48-hour turnaround, light-touch feedback only on brand-safety items.
- **DO tag @safetea.app** in the post and on-screen so we can repost.
- **DO let your audience know SafeTea is web-first** — "no app needed, just open it in your browser."

### DON'T

- **DON'T mention any real person by name** — not a celebrity, not an ex, not "this guy I dated." Pseudonyms or general framing only.
- **DON'T post real screenshots** of dating profiles, even with names blurred. The risk of identification through context is too high.
- **DON'T fearmonger.** "Be informed" not "be afraid." "Make better decisions" not "you'll be assaulted."
- **DON'T promise outcomes** SafeTea can't deliver. We're a tool; the user makes the decisions.
- **DON'T use iOS download language** ("get it on the App Store"). iOS approval is pending; the web app is the product.
- **DON'T post without disclosure.** A post without #ad / paid-partnership disclosure is out of contract — we will withhold the flat fee until corrected.
- **DON'T target minors** in any way — content, captions, or audience targeting. SafeTea is for adults dating adults.
- **DON'T re-share user content from SafeTea.** Anything you see inside the platform stays inside the platform. Period.
- **DON'T cross-promote competitors** in the same post (Tea, AWDTSG groups, dating-app safety features). Talk about SafeTea on its own merits.
