Community Guidelines

Last updated: April 1, 2026

SafeTea is a women-first dating safety community. These guidelines exist to keep everyone safe and protect the privacy of all individuals — including the people discussed in posts. By using SafeTea, you agree to follow these rules.

1. What's Allowed

You CAN post:

  • Personal dating experiences — share what happened to you in your own words
  • First name + last initial — e.g., "Jake M." or "Marcus J."
  • Conversation screenshots — with last names and profile photos blurred
  • Positive experiences — good dates, green flags, recommendations
  • Safety tips — advice for meeting people, venue suggestions, red flag awareness
  • Questions — ask the community for input on a situation

2. What's NOT Allowed

You CANNOT post:

  • Full names — first + last name is automatically blocked. Use first name + last initial.
  • Photos without consent — no photos of anyone who hasn't consented to being on SafeTea
  • Doxxing information — home address, workplace, phone number, social media handles
  • Criminal accusations without a police report — if you allege a crime, you must have filed a report
  • Harassment or threats — no targeted abuse, threats of violence, or coordinated attacks
  • Nudity or explicit content — no NSFW images or sexually explicit material
  • Content involving minors — absolutely no content about or involving anyone under 18
  • Hate speech — no content targeting race, ethnicity, religion, gender identity, sexual orientation, or disability
  • Spam or commercial promotion — no ads, scams, or promotional content

3. Consequences

Violations are reviewed by AI moderation and human moderators. Consequences escalate with severity and repeat offenses:

Level 1

Post Removed

Content removed, user notified of the violation.

Level 2

Warning

Formal warning added to your account. 3 warnings = suspension.

Level 3

30-Day Suspension

Account suspended for 30 days. All posts hidden during suspension.

Level 4

Permanent Ban

Account permanently banned. Reserved for severe or repeat violations.

4. Photo Rules

  • 10-day auto-expiry — all uploaded photos are automatically deleted after 10 days
  • Watermarked for safety — photos are invisibly watermarked to trace leaks back to the uploader
  • No screenshots — screenshot attempts are detected and may result in a warning or suspension
  • Consent required — only upload photos that you have the right to share
  • No nudity — explicit images are blocked by AI moderation and result in immediate suspension

5. Takedown Requests

If you are named in a post and want it removed, you can request a takedown through:

  • In-app: Use the "Request Removal" button on any post that mentions you
  • Online: Submit a request at getsafetea.app/dispute
  • Email: Contact support@getsafetea.app

Under the TAKE IT DOWN Act, non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII) will be removed within 48 hours of a verified request.

6. Appeals

If your post was removed or your account was suspended, you have the right to appeal:

  • You may submit one appeal per violation
  • Appeals must be filed within 7 days of the action
  • Appeals are reviewed by AI and may be escalated to a human moderator
  • You will be notified of the outcome via email and in-app message

To submit an appeal, go to Settings > Account Status in the app or email appeals@getsafetea.app.

7. AI Moderation Disclosure

SafeTea uses AI-powered moderation to review posts, detect violations, and enforce these guidelines. AI moderation works alongside human moderators to ensure safety and accuracy. All AI-flagged content is subject to human review upon appeal.
