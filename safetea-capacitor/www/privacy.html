LinkHer Privacy Policy

Effective: May 14, 2026 · Operator: Nathaniel Warner · Contact: njwarner25@gmail.com

LinkHer is a personal-safety platform built for women. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the controls you have. We collect the minimum needed to operate the safety features you turn on, and we do not sell personal data.

1. What we collect

  • Account data: email, display name, password hash, and optional phone number for SMS check-ins.
  • Trusted contacts: contacts you add for SOS, Safe Walk, and check-in features. Stored encrypted; only used when you trigger a feature that requires reaching them.
  • Location: collected only while the app is in use, only when you explicitly start Safe Walk, share live location, or open a localized Safety Brief. Not collected in the background.
  • Vault content: photos, screenshots, voice notes, and text you save to your Safety Vault. Encrypted at rest. Visible only to you.
  • Community Feed posts: safety concerns you choose to publish are visible to other LinkHer users in the same area. Posts can include text, optional images (moderated), and a coarse location.
  • Device + diagnostic data: device model, OS version, app version, crash logs, and basic usage events used to keep the app stable.
  • Push tokens: APNs/FCM device tokens used to deliver SOS alerts, Safe Walk pings, and Alessia notifications. No marketing push.

2. What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect dating profiles, romantic preferences, or matchmaking data — LinkHer is not a dating app.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, location, or Face ID until you grant permission for the specific feature that needs it.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

3. How we use it

  • To run the safety features you initiate (SOS, Safe Walk, check-ins, Safety Briefs, Alessia AI Companion).
  • To moderate content (photo moderation rejects dating-profile and third-party social media images on upload).
  • To send safety notifications (e.g., area alerts, scheduled check-in reminders).
  • To detect abuse and keep the platform safe.

4. Sharing

  • Trusted contacts you choose: receive your SOS alerts, Safe Walk updates, and check-in messages only when you trigger them.
  • Service providers: cloud hosting (Vercel), database (Postgres on Neon), push delivery (APNs/FCM), AI inference (OpenAI for Alessia). They process data on our behalf under contractual confidentiality.
  • Law enforcement: only in response to a valid legal request, or where we believe in good faith disclosure is necessary to prevent imminent harm.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal data.

5. Public safety data sources

Safety Briefs are generated from publicly available sources including the FBI National Incident-Based Reporting System (NIBRS), OpenStreetMap venue context, and the National Weather Service. We do not cross-reference your identity with these public datasets.

6. Retention

  • Vault content is kept until you delete it.
  • Trusted-contact records are kept until you remove the contact or delete your account.
  • Location pings tied to a Safe Walk session are deleted within 30 days of the session ending.
  • Account data is deleted within 30 days of an account-deletion request.

7. Your controls

  • Permissions: revoke camera, photo, location, microphone, contacts, or Face ID access at any time in iOS Settings.
  • Account deletion: in-app under Settings → Account → Delete Account. Email njwarner25@gmail.com for assistance.
  • Data export: request a copy of your account data by emailing the address above.

8. Children

LinkHer is rated 17+ and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Security

All traffic is HTTPS. Vault content is encrypted at rest. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. We follow industry-standard practices but no system is 100% secure.

10. International transfers

LinkHer is operated from the United States. By using the app you consent to processing of your data in the U.S.

11. Changes

If we materially change this policy we will notify you in-app before the change takes effect.

12. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints: njwarner25@gmail.com.
