## The 30-second "should I follow this account?" filter

Before clicking follow, eyeball the account for ~30 seconds and check ALL of these:

- [ ] **Active in last 30 days?** Posted within the month. Dead accounts waste a follow slot.
- [ ] **Follower count in your target band?** 5kâ€“500k for most niches; smaller is OK for hyperlocal or AWDTSG.
- [ ] **Female-skewing audience?** Scan the recent comment section. If 80%+ commenters look male, skip.
- [ ] **Last 5 posts on-topic?** Quick check â€” the account should clearly be about your niche, not a general lifestyle account that occasionally mentions dating.
- [ ] **No deal-breakers in bio?** No pyramid schemes / MLM, no "I'll teach you to seduce her in 7 days" energy, no anti-feminist positioning, no obvious bot account.
- [ ] **Not a direct competitor's official account?** (Tea app, Lulu, etc. â€” DO follow for monitoring, but mark them in your tracker)

If any single box is unchecked â†’ skip. Better to follow 25 high-fit accounts than 50 mediocre ones.
