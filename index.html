FCRA Compliance Notice

SafeTea is not a Consumer Reporting Agency (CRA) as defined by the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA), 15 U.S.C. § 1681 et seq. The information available through SafeTea's safety tools is gathered from publicly available sources and is provided on an "as-is" basis for personal awareness and community safety purposes only.

You may not use SafeTea to:

  • Make decisions about employment, tenant screening, or hiring
  • Determine eligibility for credit, insurance, or financial services
  • Evaluate individuals for any purpose governed by the FCRA
  • Stalk, harass, or intimidate any individual
  • Violate any applicable local, state, or federal law

By using SafeTea's background check and registry lookup features, you certify that you will not use the information for any prohibited purpose. SafeTea does not verify the accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of public records and assumes no liability for errors or omissions. For official records, contact the relevant government agency directly.
