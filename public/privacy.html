Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

SafeTea is built on the principle that women deserve to date safely without sacrificing their privacy. We collect only what's necessary to provide our safety features, and we never sell your data.

TL;DR: We don't sell your data. We don't show your real name. We don't track you outside the app. Your safety information stays private.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address — for login and important notifications (Name Watch alerts, safety check-ins)
  • Display name — a pseudonym you choose, never your real name
  • City — to connect you with your local community
  • Phone number (optional) — for SMS-based login and Date Check-In notifications

Content You Create

  • Community posts and replies
  • Name Watch entries (stored privately, visible only to you)
  • Date Check-In details (shared only with your chosen contacts)
  • Red Flag Scanner conversations (analyzed by AI, not stored permanently)
  • Photo Verification photo uploads (analyzed and discarded — we don't store your photos)

Automatically Collected

  • IP address — for rate limiting and abuse prevention only
  • Device type and browser — for rendering the site correctly
  • Approximate location — only when you explicitly use "Use My Location" for area alerts

How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption — all data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Anonymous by design — no real names are required or displayed. Your display name is a pseudonym.
  • No screenshots — our mobile app blocks screen capture to protect shared experiences
  • Watermarked photos — uploaded photos are digitally watermarked to trace unauthorized distribution
  • Rate limiting — API endpoints are protected against abuse and brute-force attacks
  • Passwords — hashed with bcrypt, never stored in plain text

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide safety features (Name Watch, Date Check-In, Red Flag Scanner, Photo Verification)
  • To send you safety-critical notifications (Name Watch matches, check-in reminders)
  • To moderate content and enforce community guidelines
  • To improve our AI analysis accuracy
  • To scan user-generated content for policy violations using automated AI systems (see AI Moderation Disclosure below)

AI Moderation Disclosure

SafeTea may utilize automated systems, including artificial intelligence, to identify potentially harmful or policy-violating content. These AI systems scan content for profanity, personal information, threats, and other policy violations. AI moderation is advisory in nature only — automated systems do not create, modify, or contribute to user content. Flagged content may be reviewed by our team. The use of AI moderation does not constitute editorial control over user content.

We never: sell your data to advertisers, share your identity with other users, give law enforcement access without a valid warrant, or use your data for targeted advertising.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Vercel — hosting and serverless functions (US-based)
  • Neon/Vercel Postgres — database storage (US-based)
  • OpenAI — AI analysis for Red Flag Scanner and Photo Verification. Conversations are sent to OpenAI for analysis but are not used to train their models.
  • SendGrid — transactional emails (Name Watch alerts, welcome emails)
  • Stripe — payment processing for subscriptions. We never see or store your credit card number.
  • Twilio — SMS authentication and Date Check-In notifications

Data Retention

  • Community posts — retained until deleted by you or a moderator
  • Red Flag Scanner results — summary stored for 90 days, conversation text not permanently stored
  • Catfish scan photos — analyzed in memory and discarded immediately after scanning
  • Crime alerts — retained for 90 days (rolling window from public data sources)
  • Account data — retained until you delete your account
  • Rate limit records — automatically purged after the time window expires

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — view your profile, posts, and Name Watch list anytime
  • Delete your account — removes all your data permanently (Settings > Delete Account)
  • Export your data — contact support@getsafetea.app
  • Correct your information — edit your profile anytime in Settings
  • Opt out of non-essential emails — toggle notifications in Settings

Age Requirements

SafeTea is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor is using SafeTea, contact us at support@getsafetea.app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
