Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

SafeTea ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address, display name, city, and password (hashed).
  • User-generated content: Posts, comments, referrals, and reports you submit to the community.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and interaction timestamps to improve the platform.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and screen size for compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the SafeTea platform.
  • To enable community features like posting, searching, and messaging.
  • To enforce our Community Guidelines and moderate content.
  • To send safety alerts relevant to your city.
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns.

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We do not share your identity with other users — all posts are anonymous.
  • We do not use your data for targeted advertising.
  • We do not require or store your real name.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and access controls. While no system is 100% secure, we are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can request account deletion at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your personal data will be removed within 30 days. Anonymous posts may remain for community safety purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.
  • Request a copy of your data.
  • Close your account at any time.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or an in-app notice.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@getsafetea.app.
